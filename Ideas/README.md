# Ideas

This folder contains early-stage thoughts, possibilities, and brainstorms. Ideas are embryonic - they haven't evolved enough to become projects or clearly fit into an area of responsibility.

## What Belongs Here

- Quick captures: "Idea for my next blog post..."
- Exploratory thoughts: "What if I built a tool that..."
- Brainstorms: "Been thinking about starting a podcast..."
- Possibilities that don't require immediate action

## Idea Lifecycle

Ideas typically follow this path:

1. **Raw** - Just captured, unfiltered thought
2. **Developing** - Adding context, exploring possibilities
3. **Promoted** - Evolved into a Project or added to an Area
4. **Archived** - Not pursuing, but worth keeping

## Ideas vs. Other Categories

- **Ideas** = "I might want to do this" (possibility)
- **Projects** = "I'm committed to doing this" (goal with deadline)
- **Areas** = "I'm actively maintaining this" (ongoing responsibility)

## Usage Tips

- Capture fast - don't overthink the structure
- Review periodically to promote promising ideas
- Connect ideas to discover patterns
- Archive ideas you're not pursuing to keep this folder focused
