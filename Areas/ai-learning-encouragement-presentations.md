# AI Learning Encouragement Presentations

## Scope
A series of weekly half-hour recorded AI presentations designed to encourage collaboration, dialog, and participation in AI. This area encompasses presentations at multiple skill levels (Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced), demonstrating AI techniques and promoting community engagement.

## Standards
- **Frequency**: Weekly presentations
- **Duration**: 30 minutes per session
- **Format**: Recorded presentations
- **Coverage**: Topics span Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels
- **Approach**: Never goes too deep due to time constraints
- **Quality**: Demonstrations are clear and practical
- **Engagement**: Encourages attendee participation and project collaboration

## Key Notes
- Focus on demonstrating practical AI techniques
- Promotes attendees working together on collaborative projects
- Projects developed can be presented/demoed at future presentations
- Emphasizes dialog and discussion
- Specifically designed to encourage hesitant individuals to jump in and try AI
- Balance between education and encouragement
